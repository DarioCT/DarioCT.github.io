Time-Series Problems

Time Series is an ordered sequence of values of a variable at equally spaced time intervals. Time series data often arise when monitoring industrial processes or tracking corporate business metrics. The essential difference between modelling data via time series methods or using the other methods. Time series analysis accounts for the fact that data points taken over time may have an internal structure (such as autocorrelation, trend or seasonal variation) that should be accounted for.

▷ Electrical Load Prediction

This project aims to create a deep learning neural network model capable of predicting electrical load from past historical data and categorical data like period of the day, weekday, and date. Electrical load prediction is important for both national economies and individual power utilities and even smaller power companies. Reliable load forecasting makes securing electricity supply and scheduling easier as contributing to primary energy saving by allowing better renewable energy integration. The data used encompasses more than 7 years of historical data with 256988 electrical load instances.

Key Words: Time-Series, Deep Neural Network, Data Preprocessing, Feature Engineering, Feature Extraction, RMSE, MAPE

Created using: Python (Keras, SciKit Learn, NumPy, Pandas, Seaborn)
